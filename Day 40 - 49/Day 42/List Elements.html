Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the milk mixture.
  3. Add in the eggs and mix well.
  4. Gradually add the bread flour and mix until it forms a dough.
  5. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  6. Transfer the dough into a large bowl, cover it with plastic wrap, and let it rise for 2 hours or until doubled in size.
  7. After the dough has risen, roll it out into a large rectangle on a floured surface.
  8. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the dark brown sugar and ground cinnamon.
  9. Spread the cinnamon sugar mixture evenly over the rolled-out dough.
  10. Roll the dough into a tight log, starting from one long edge.
  11. Cut the rolled dough into 3cm sections or shawarma-sized portions.
  12. Place the dough sections flat into a baking tray.
  13. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  14. Bake the shawarma rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they are lightly browned.
